People Say...

People say you’re a play girl,
yet I choose you.

People say you’re too flat, not a good choice,
yet I choose you.

People say you look older,
yet I choose you.

People say you’re not friendly,
yet I choose you.

People say you’re complicated,
yet I choose you.

People say you’re stubborn,
yet I choose you.

People say I deserve better than you,
yet I choose you.

People say… People say…
But I don’t listen.
Because every word fades,
and the only truth I carry is this:
I choose you.

"I Choose You"
